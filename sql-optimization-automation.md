# Case Study: SQL Process Optimization & ETL Automation

## 📌 Background
While working within the Telecommunications sector, I transitioned from handling repetitive manual data tasks to supervising workgroups by identifying opportunities for technical automation. This project focused on streamlining how data was extracted and processed for various business departments.

## ⚠️ The Challenge
The organization relied on manual data extraction from multiple, fragmented databases. This process was:
* **Time-Consuming:** High-level analysts were spending 40% of their week on manual data manipulation.
* **Prone to Latency:** Reports were often delayed due to the complexity of the joins required across diverse tables.
* **Inconsistent:** Manual extraction led to slight variations in KPIs depending on who ran the report.

## 🛠️ The Solution
I developed a library of efficient, automated **SQL stored procedures** to standardize and accelerate data delivery.
* **Complex Query Development:** Wrote advanced SQL queries to join and aggregate data from multiple enterprise databases into a unified reporting layer.
* **Stored Procedures:** Created and maintained stored procedures in **Oracle SQL** that automated the extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) of data.
* **Scalability:** Built the queries to be modular, allowing different business departments (Finance, Marketing, Operations) to pull the specific data they needed without writing new code.
* **Self-Starter Initiative:** Took the initiative to learn advanced PL/SQL during my internship to solve these bottlenecks, eventually leading to my promotion to a supervisory role.

## 📈 Results & Impact
* **Operational Efficiency:** Reduced manual data processing time by **40%** across multiple departments.
* **Technical Speed:** Achieved a **25% reduction** in total data processing time for large-scale reports.
* **Data Integrity:** Established a "Single Version of Truth" for KPIs, eliminating discrepancies in stakeholder reporting.

## 🧰 Skills Demonstrated
* **Database:** Oracle SQL, PL/SQL, Stored Procedures, ETL.
* **Technical Leadership:** Mentoring junior analysts and supervising workgroup data standards.
* **Tools:** SQL Developer, Microsoft Excel (Advanced), Enterprise Data Warehouses.
