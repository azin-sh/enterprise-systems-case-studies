# Case Study: Data-Driven Marketing Strategy & Visualization

## 📌 Background
In my role as a Data Analyst, I collaborated with the Marketing department to transform raw customer feedback and demographic data into a strategic asset. The goal was to better understand customer behavior to improve the effectiveness of promotional campaigns.

## ⚠️ The Challenge
The marketing team had access to large amounts of customer data, but it was stored across multiple sources (Isolated CRMs and Excel sheets). This made it difficult to:
* Identify which customer segments were most likely to respond to specific offers.
* Track the relationship between customer satisfaction and long-term loyalty.
* Visualize trends over time to adjust marketing budgets.

## 🛠️ The Solution
I led the data integration and visualization efforts to create a "Single Source of Truth" for the marketing team.
* **Data Integration:** Used **SQL** and **Python** to extract and clean data from multiple databases, creating a consolidated view of the customer journey.
* **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Identified patterns in customer feedback and spending habits using statistical analysis.
* **Tableau Dashboards:** Developed interactive dashboards that allowed the Marketing team to "drill down" into specific demographics (Age, Region, Service Plan).
* **Feedback Loop:** Built a system to categorize qualitative customer feedback into quantitative scores for easier tracking.

## 📈 Results & Impact
* **Targeted Growth:** Enabled the marketing team to launch highly specific campaigns, resulting in a **15% rise in targeted marketing success**.
* **Customer Retention:** Identified key pain points in the customer journey, leading to a **30% increase in customer satisfaction scores**.
* **Business Intelligence:** Reduced the time spent on manual reporting by providing real-time access to performance KPIs.

## 🧰 Skills Demonstrated
* **Tools:** Tableau, SQL, Python (Pandas/NumPy).
* **Techniques:** Data Visualization, Customer Segmentation, ETL (Extract, Transform, Load).
* **Soft Skills:** Cross-functional collaboration, Stakeholder management, Strategic planning.
